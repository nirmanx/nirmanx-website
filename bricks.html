Steel Buying Guide

Fe-500D vs Fe-550D?

Fe-500D is standard for residential construction. Fe-550D is stronger — use for high rise & commercial buildings.

How much steel?

For 1000 sqft house approximately 4–5 tonnes of TMT bars needed. Consult structural engineer for exact quantity.

Check Authenticity

Always check BIS mark on bars. Look for brand name, grade, and manufacturer code on each bar.
