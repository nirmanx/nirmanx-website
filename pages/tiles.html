Tile Buying Guide

Vitrified vs Ceramic?

Vitrified tiles are stronger, less porous & easier to maintain. Best for floors. Ceramic tiles are cheaper & good for walls.

How many tiles?

Calculate your room area + add 10% extra for wastage. Use our calculator on the home page for exact quantity.

Size Selection

600×600mm looks spacious in large rooms. 300×300mm good for bathrooms. 800×800mm for premium look.

Safe Delivery

All tiles packed in cartons with foam padding. Delivered carefully to avoid breakage. Inspection before unloading.
